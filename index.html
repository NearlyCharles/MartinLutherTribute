Martin Luther

Father of the Protestants

Here's a time line of Martin Luthers life:

  • 1483 - Born in Eisleben, Germany.
  • 1501 - Martin luther attends the University of Erfurt at 17.
  • 1505 - Luther recieves his master's degree and shortly after has his encounter with a lightning bolt where he dedicates his life as a monk. Fifthteen days later Martin Luther entered St. Augustine's Monastery in Erfurt.
  • 1507 - Luther is ordained by the Bishop of Bradenburg Jerome Schultz.
  • 1508 - He begins teaching theology at the University of Wittenburg and receives two bachelor's degrees. One in biblical studies and one in sentences in 1509.
  • 1512 - Luther is awarded his Doctor of Theology and was seated as chair of theology at the University of Wittenburg, a position he held for the rest of his career.
